The Rules:
A random word is generated and each letter is outputted
as a blank. You try to guess the letters by typing them,
one at a time, and pressing the guess letter button. If you
guess the right letter than it appears in the word; if you
guess an incorrect letter than it is shown in the wrong guesses
and the image fades a little. If the image completly fades out
then you lose; if you guess all the letters in the word
before the picture fades then you win.
