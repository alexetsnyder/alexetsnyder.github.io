The Rules
A random word is chosen and each letter is displayed as a blank.
Choose different letters by clicking on the letters on the screen.
Once a letter is chosen it disappears. You must spell out the word
to win, but if you make more than six wrong guesses than you lose.
Each time you make a wrong guess more pieces are added to the stick
person on the gallows. When the stick person is completely drawn you lose.
